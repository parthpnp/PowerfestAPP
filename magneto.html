Magneto
Magneto

Well everyone must have played the 'Javelin Throw' video game in their childhood. The javelin was part of the pentathlon of the Ancient Olympic Games beginning in 708 BC, in two disciplines, distance and target throw. Based on this concept Techfest presents the competition 'Magneto' giving a modern touch to the game where the player has to command a hand gesture controlled Robot that will throw the ball at the target in the arena.

  • FAQs

    What is the registration fee?
    There is no registration fee for this competition.

    Who can participate ?
    Student from any college or university with a valid ID card can participate in the competition.

    How to register ?
    Visit www.techfest.org -> Competition -> Robotron -> Magneto -> Register

    How many persons can be there in one team?
    4

    Can I register in more than one competition?
    Yes, you can participate in more than one competition. it is recommended to focus on one competition as there can be some chances of slot clash.

    Why do I need to submit abstract?
    The abstract submission is mandatory for those teams who need accommodation inside IIT Bombay campus. Since, we have limited accommodation available inside IIT campus, prefernce for accommodation will be given to those teams who submits the abstract. In all other cases, teams will have to take care of their own accommodation. For more information about accommodation, please visit www.techfest.org/hospitality.

    Our team doesn't need accommodation inside IIT Bombay campus then, do we still need to submit the abstract?
    No, it only for those teams who needs accommodation inside IIT campus.
