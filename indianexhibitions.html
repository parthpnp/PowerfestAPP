Indian Exhibitions


Over the years, Exhibitions at Techfest is bolstering to spread a spirit of creativity and a sense of competition among the budding geniuses of the world. Techfest takes a leap forward to promote research and development in India this time around.

This year Techfest, IIT Bombay is launching a public exhibition where in you can get a chance to exhibit your work to the most diverse and intellectual crowd in India of corporate personnels, students and professors. Exhibits in the following categories are welcome.

  • Robotics
  • Electronics
  • Engineering
  • Architecture
  • Fine Art
  • Design
  • Automobiles
  • Pure Science
  • Interaction
  • Energy

The exhibit must be original or developed by you. The top 25 exhibits overall would be selected and given an opportunity to be a part of exhibitions at Techfest, IIT Bombay.

Send us the video/images of your exhibit along with one page abstract at praneeth.allada@techfest.org by 25th of November. Videos are preferred. In case of photos, make sure that you are sending images from all the views.

There is no registration fees for the submission. The final selection will be done by Techfest, IIT Bombay.

Note:

  • In case your exhibit has a patent, please attach patent details in the mail.
  • If you are a company or an organisation then charges may apply. Please contact organiser for further information.
