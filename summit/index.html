INTERNET OF THINGS

ABOUT IoT

Internet of Things is a trending and great possible investment as it is the next big revolution to be seen. In short it is often referred to as IoT. It deals with the network of physical objects or "things" embedded with electronics, software, sensors, and network connectivity, which enables these objects to collect and exchange data. The Internet of Things allows objects to be sensed and controlled remotely across existing network infrastructure, creating opportunities for more direct integration between the physical world and computer-based systems.The advantages and capabilities of IoT are endless. The interconnection of these embedded devices (including smart objects), is expected to usher in automation in nearly all fields, while also enabling advanced applications like a Smart Grid, and expanding to the areas such as Smart Cities. But all great things sure do have loopholes. With everything connected there is a greater risk to privacy and data security. This is a field where you can expect as much growth as the IoT progresses. IoT isn't a distant future, it is being achieved as we speak. The Summit will provide an ideal introduction if you want to pursue it as a career or want to integrate it into your work.

Why Attend

All major researchers estimate there will be tens of billions of devices - computers, smartphones, tablets, and sensors - connected to the Internet by 2020. This number will continue to grow at a rapid pace for the next several decades.

With major technology companies and startups seriously embracing IoT strategies, The Inaugural IoT Summit will bring together corporate, entrepreneurs and investors alike, under one roof, to explore how the Internet of Things means business.

The IoT Summit is a must attend event for companies and organisations that want to understand how their business will both be affected by IoT and how they may benefit from it. Attendees will learn what is going on, contribute to the discussions and ensure that their enterprise is "IoT-Ready"!

For Students

It would facilitate an introduction to IoT,which being rapidly adopted all over the world, is very much on its way to becoming the mainstay of the tech world. It’s the perfect opportunity for all students with a basic set of coding skills to check this thriving career option. Following are few things to look forward to:

  • A basic introduction to IoT’s standing in the industry through keynote and panel discussions.
  • An opportunity of networking session with professionals and like minded individuals which would set the foundation of your views on IoT and possibly your career by personal interactions and intellectual discussions with people of similar interest to increase your own knowledge base.
  • A Workshop with a hands on approach on the various IoT applications available. It will be an industrial standard workshop by Texas Instruments requiring just a basic knowledge of the coding. It’s on the TI CC3200 platform using a variety of tools.

For Professionals

The IoT summit is must attend event for all the professionals from the various IT/Software cells of various organizations across all stratas of industry. Also techies with a sound coding background who would like to know about this phenomenon as a hobbyist or as a part of a startup, would gain a lot from adapting to IoT. It would allow one to hear from the stalwarts of the industry and get the real picture where it stands in India and in the World. The networking session would greatly help you as you would not only get to interact with like minded professionals but also the various Investors and IoT bigwigs attending the Summit.
