Technovoltz

Heroes and ordinary humans in Greece frequently discovered that things were not what they appeared to be. Their actions stemmed from recognizable passions such as pride, jealousy and the thirst for revenge and above all the need to gain power and prove their supremacy. The underlying moral principle was that the gods rewarded honorable behavior and obedience, and people who dishonored themselves or defied the gods usually paid a high price.

The Battle of Thermopylae was fought between an alliance of Greek city-states, led by King Leonidas of Sparta and the Persian Empire of Xerxes over a course of three days, during the second Persian invasion of Greece. Xerxes had amassed a huge army and navy and set out to conquer all of Greece. The Athenian general Themistocles had proposed that the allied Greeks block the advance of the Persian army at the pass of Thermopylae and simultaneously block the Persian navy at the Straits of Artemisium.

Now the new world has a time machine and you are given the opportunity to write the history anew. King Leonidas has decided to develop a strategy to protect his kingdom from the Persian invasion. He calls for the council consisting of aristocrats and generals to discuss the stratagem against the peril the kingdom faces and a concrete plan is agreed upon. Its success highly depends on you and your skills to live up to the king's expectation. After the meeting, Oplítibot (Block Warrior) goes on with his army to face the Persians in battle ground, Mystikóbot(Maze Runner) infiltrates the enemy base and tries to find a way to the treasure of the Persians without which they will stop the war and Battle Code thinks of diplomatic strategies of making peace with the Persians.

The die has been set, will history repeat itself and the mutual destruction be ensured or will the strategist from the future, you, turn the tables and minimize the losses?

You are faced with three great perils before you can set forth the banner of victory.

  • ABOUT WILD CARD QUALIFIERS

    As a part of National Outreach Program called Technovoltz, Techfest conducted three competitions in four different cities of India. Top three teams from each competition have been given direct entry into Grand Finale. Those teams who couldn't able to compete/win at any of the zonal qualifiers can compete in the Wild Card qualifiers. Winners of Wild Card qualifiers will compete with the winners of zonal qualifiers in the Grand Finale. Registrations for the Wild Card qualifiers are open now.

  • VENUES

    The zonal qualifiers were successfully conducted at the following venues. Top three teams of each competition i.e Maze Runner, Block warrior, Battle Code will qualify for the Grand Finale which will be held at IIT Bombay from 26th - 28th December 2015.
